" ประเทศญี่ปุ่น (Japan:日本) "




A tourist attraction that many people want to try visiting at least once. It is a country that has a fascinating charm. Cutting-edge technology
Famous for both food Beautiful cultural and natural attractions You can travel around the country comfortably and quickly using the Shinkansen. Currently, Thai tourists can travel to Japan for 15 days without applying for a visa.

Japan has many interesting places. But the top tourist attractions in Japan that Thai people like to visit include Mount Fuji (Fujisan). The surrounding atmosphere is beautiful and changes every season. Shinjuku is a shopping area that everyone who goes to Tokyo must always stop by. Tokyo Tower(Tokyo Tower) It is like a symbol of the capital of Japan. Sai Water Temple (Kiyomizu-dera) Not far away, there is a place where Thai people often go to take pictures with the giant Glico sign in the area.Dotonbori in Osaka
